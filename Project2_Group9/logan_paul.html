About Logan Paul

Logan Paul is a social media influencer, youtuber, entrepreneur and professional wrestler. He started off as a viner back in 2013, started his first youtube channel "TheOfficialLoganPaul" and then later "Logan Paul Vlogs" in 2015, which as of 2023 has recieved 23.6 million subscribers. Logan Paul is also a wrestler and performs part-time for the World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) and is a former "WWE United States Champion". Logan Paul stated a podcast in November 2018 called "Impaulsive" which has over 4 million subscribers on youtube.

Logan Paul also ventured into the amateur boxing arena with his first fight being with the youtuber KSI which he lost. He would later have a rematch with KSI which he lost, again. Paul would also later go on to have a boxing match with mixed martial artist Dilon Danis which he won due to Dilon Danis being disqualified. Logan Paul has also acted in a few movies such as "The Thinning", "The Thinning: New World Order" and "Airplane Mode".

Logan Paul would also later on be the co-founder of "Prime" which is a range of energy drinks, drink mixes and sports drinks along with internet personality and sidemen member KSI. Logan Paul teamed up with KSI again as well as famous internet personality Mr Beast to launch lunchly which was marketed to be better then Kraft Heinz's lunchables. The product was announced on September 16, 2024.
