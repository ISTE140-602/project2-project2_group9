Born on June 19, 1993, Olajide Olayinka Williams "JJ" Olatunji is a professional boxer, musician, and social media influencer from England who goes by the online handle KSI. Prime Hydration, XIX Vodka, Sidemen Clothing, the restaurant chain Sides, the cereal brand Best Breakfasts, and the snack brand Lunchly are all owned by him. He is also the CEO of Misfits Boxing and a co-founder of the YouTube group the Sidemen. In 2009, he created his primary YouTube account and gained popularity by making gaming commentary videos around the FIFA video game series. Later, he expanded his output to include comedy-style videos and vlogs. By October 2022, his three YouTube channels had more than 10 billion video views and more than 41 million subscribers.

He was included in Forbes' 30 Under 30 Europe Entertainment list in 2021. He has also dabbled in acting, making an appearance in the 2016 British comedy Laid in America. He was the focus of two documentaries: KSI: In Real Life (2023), which followed his life from June 2021 to October 2022 on Amazon Prime Video, and KSI: Can't Lose (2018), which followed the events leading up to his first boxing battle. Dissimulation, his first studio album, was released in 2020 and debuted at number two on the UK Albums Chart. He has been making rap music since 2011. All Over the Place, his second studio album, came out in 2021 and peaked at number one.
